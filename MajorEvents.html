Major Events






"RoboEminence," a dynamic event featuring a range of robot demonstrations, including Robo Fire, Optical Avoiding, and Voice Control models. The event was inaugurated by DRDO scientist Dr. Vidya Shankar Pandey, who delivered an insightful presentation on DRDO's achievements and inspired students with an interactive session. Dean Professor A.K. Singh and convenor Mr. Kamlesh Tripathi welcomed attendees. With over 150 participants, "RoboEminence" offered an engaging platform for students to explore cutting-edge robotics, concluding with the national anthem.
The 8-day robotics and animation lecture series was transformative for students, covering foundational principles to advanced applications. The program offered a holistic understanding of both theoretical concepts and practical implementation, exposing students to the latest industry trends. With an extended duration for in-depth exploration and hands-on exercises, participants left with a solid foundation in robotics and automation. The series instilled confidence in applying newfound expertise to real-world scenarios, positioning students as adept contributors to the evolving fields of technology and creativity.
The introductory workshop on robotics and animation was a transformative experience for students, guided by skilled instructors. It seamlessly blended theoretical concepts with hands-on exposure to cutting-edge technologies, fostering a comprehensive understanding. The interactive approach, involving testing and animating robots, ignited creativity and cultivated problem-solving skills. This immersive learning served as a catalyst, empowering students with knowledge and skills that extend beyond the classroom, paving the way for future innovation in the exciting fields of robotics and animation.
